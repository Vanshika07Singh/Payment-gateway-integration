Our Vision

We aspire to a world where all individuals – particularly those in need and those who are displaced – live a life of health and dignity.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of suffering and displaced people around the world. We provide emergency and disaster relief in the immediate aftermath of acute disasters and work towards the rehabilitation and reconstruction of afflicted communities. We work on the frontlines to provide immediate and long-term relief to those who need it most.
